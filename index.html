The Person

Built on depth, not just breadth

I have spent 14 years at the intersection where databases meet business outcomes. From classified government networks in Abu Dhabi to FinTech platforms processing millions of transactions, from 1,000-node Cassandra clusters at Visa to 65-cluster migrations at Contentstack — I operate in environments where database failures are not an option.

My approach: understand the business problem first, then select the right technology. I have worked across MongoDB, Cassandra, Aurora PostgreSQL, MySQL, ClickHouse, Redis, Neo4j, DynamoDB, Oracle, and SQL Server. Equally effective as a hands-on platform engineer, solutions architect, pre-sales technical advisor, and engineering leader.

Currently building AI-assisted database operations — personal agents, vector search, agentic infrastructure provisioning. The future of database operations is not manual.
