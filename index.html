The Person

Built on depth,
not just breadth

I have spent 14 years at the intersection where databases meet business outcomes. From classified government networks in Abu Dhabi to FinTech platforms processing millions of transactions, from 1,000-node Cassandra clusters at Visa to 65-cluster migrations at high-growth SaaS — I have operated in environments where database failures are not an option.

My approach is simple: understand the business problem first, then select the right technology. I have worked across MongoDB, Cassandra, Aurora PostgreSQL, MySQL, ClickHouse, Redis, Neo4j, DynamoDB, Oracle, and SQL Server. Each has its place. The skill is knowing which place.

I am equally effective as a hands-on platform engineer building automation pipelines, a solutions architect designing multi-database architectures for AI workloads, a pre-sales technical advisor running proof-of-value engagements, and an engineering leader building teams from scratch.

Currently exploring AI-assisted database operations — building personal agents, evaluating vector search architectures, and pushing toward agentic infrastructure provisioning. Because the future of database operations is not manual.

Contentstack Inc.
Senior Manager — Database Administration · Bangalore, India

  • Led proof-of-concept for MongoDB Atlas Vector Search for AI-driven content generation — evaluated Pinecone and Milvus as alternatives, recommended Atlas based on superior retrieval performance; shipped to production
  • Built and mentored a database team from scratch — two junior engineers hired as interns now lead teams
